What Awaits You

By Joining Our Open Day,
You Will Experience…

A day filled with discovery, mentorship, culture, and inspiration — all in one place.

🔧
Explore Engineering Fields
Understand the differences between Civil, Electrical, Mechanical, Marine, and Computer Engineering. Learn about future career opportunities in each discipline.
👨‍🏫
Meet Lecturers & Professors
Talk directly with experienced academics, ask your questions, and get valuable advice about studies, research, and future engineering trends.
🎭
Cultural & Creative Activities
Enjoy cultural events and performances that showcase student talent. Engineering is not only about studying — it's about creativity and fun too!
🏆
Competitions & Events
Learn about robotics, hackathons, and sports events where you can improve your skills and showcase your talents beyond the classroom.
🔬
Visit Modern Laboratories
Explore advanced labs and workshops equipped with modern technology where students gain hands-on practical experience every day.
🗣️
Talk with Senior Students
Have friendly chats with seniors and learn about real campus life, studies, and hostel life from people who've lived through it.
